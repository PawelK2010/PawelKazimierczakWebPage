Science Symposium

First Innovation Award for a low-cost rehabilitation solution

I participated in the European School Science Symposium and won the First Innovation Award. My project focused on making wrist rehabilitation more accessible with a low-cost glove designed to support recovery exercises.

The final report explains the motivation behind the idea, the glove design, the testing process, and the results collected from several users over four weeks. The goal was to create a simpler and more affordable tool for people recovering from wrist injuries.
